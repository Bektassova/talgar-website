Akbulak RESORT

Located about 40 kilometres east of Almaty at a height of 1,600 metres above sea level.We invite you to relax in nature with your children, gain strength, ride a cable car with stunning views, go hiking and horseback riding with an experienced instructor, or enjoy sports programs: HIIT, Full bidy, stretch, crossFit, yoga, Physical jerks, Tabat



Accommodation in comfortable hotel rooms with mountain views Delicious and varied dishes in the Alatau restaurant As well as an assortment of kebabs from our chef, taste fresh saumal and kumis



Ascents on the cable car T1 and T2, with stunning views of the Trans-Ili Alatau mountains Professional swimming pool 15*25 with sauna, SPA treatments, massage Wellness program Ak Bulak Health slim fit



SKI STADIUM

Sledding and skiing trails and an ice rink are available for visitors. A unique ropeway 3,700 metres long helps visitors climb to ski and snowboard trails. Trails are 5,000 metres long with a maximum slope angle of 55 degrees. The highest point in the ski area is 2,700 metres above sea level.The ski stadium includes slopes of varying degrees of difficulty
